Things To Do In San Diego

Year round the weather for San Diego doesn't really change. Sometimes I take for granted how beautiful it is. I don't need different types of clothes for the four seasons. Most of the time I am able to wear a t-shirt and shorts on a winter day, while enjoying the sunset at the beach. Here are my favorite places to go to:

Price - Free, Location - Sunset Cliffs
This place is so beautiful. It is in between Hill Street and Monaco Street. A friend and I originally wanted to explore the open ceiling cave at Sunset Cliffs. In order to get to it we had to climb down the rock formations and ended up at the tidepools. So come to this place when it is low tide! Also don't forget to bring extra shoes and socks because you will get your feet wet.
